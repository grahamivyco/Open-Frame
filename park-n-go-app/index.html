FAQs

Can I cancel my parking space?

Yes. You must cancel 2 hours prior to the event in order to get a full refund. You will receive your refund in 2–3 business days.

How do I find where I parked?

After you reserve a parking space, an email with parking directions and your section info is sent to you. You can also view it any time under “My Parking.”

Do I have to park in my assigned lot?

No. You are not required to park in your reserved section, but reserving guarantees a spot in that lot for your event.

How do I add a payment method?

Head to your Profile and tap the Wallet menu to add, remove, or update a card.
